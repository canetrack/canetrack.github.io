On the Job records - Easy with CaneTrack

BMP Compliance in the palm of your hand.

Canegrowers - Do you need help with record keeping for complying with government regulations and Sugar Mill quality assurance requirements? CaneTrack is for you.

CaneTrack is a web app designed for desktop and mobile use, allowing you to record your farming activities, so you can easily make a permanent record of anything an auditor might ask for.

- Enter data quickly via computer, tablet or smartphone.
- Data stored and automatically backed up on a central server.
- Set up to suit your requirements eg blocks, equipment.

Farming activities can be recorded in the field immediately after the job is completed, instead of having to remember and record details later.

What CaneTrack Can Do For You

Record keeping has long been a burden if not a barrier to farmers meeting compliance requirements and maximizing profit by keeping costs to a minimum and maximizing production.

Instead of having to keep records in books or filling in record-keeping templates of farming activities, the CaneTrack app allows on the job input on your phone or tablet.

CaneTrack is based on the Smartcane BMP model of record keeping and has been developed in consultation with CANEGROWERS and extensively trialled by canegrowers.
